Information of the Action Week

The Action Week is a campaign against the current far-right government led by Petteri Orpo. During the Action Week, we march together for mental health, against cuts, and bid farewell to the far-right government!

What? Where? Why?

The Action Week is a campaign during which we fight against the policies of the far-right government and bid farewell to the blue-black government.

The Action Week will take place from November 27 to December 3, 2023, and during the week, we will organize the following protests

- Opening protest of the Action Week, November 27th 2023, from Senate Square to the Parliament House
- Protest for mental health, November 28th 2023, in front of the Parliament House
- March against cuts, December 2nd 2023, from Senate Square to the Parliament House
- Farewell to the government march and street block, December 3rd 2023 from Senate Square to the Parliament House
In addition to the protests, we will also organize 7 slow marches in Helsinki, one for each day of the Action Week.

We are organizing the Action Week because we oppose the inhumane policies of Petteri Orpo's far-right government. We demand humane policies and the resignation of the current government.

Join us to bid farewell to the far-right government!
